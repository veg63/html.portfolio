The Best Movies According to Mark

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Bandits

This is my favorite action-comedy movie. An hilarious action comedy. Stars Bruce Willis, Billy Bob Thornton & Kate Blanchett.

IT

This is my favorite horror movie. Excellent story line and special effects. Based on the novel by Stephen King.

Inside Man

This is my favorite crime-thriller movie. Excellent story line & plot. Stars Jodie Foster, Clive Owen & Denzel Washington.
